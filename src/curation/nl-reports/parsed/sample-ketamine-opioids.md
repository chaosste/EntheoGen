Ketamine × serotonergic_opioids

Core interpretation: This combination warrants caution due to additive sedation and possible serotonergic adverse effects, but there is limited direct clinical evidence.

Mechanism: NMDA-mediated dissociation from ketamine may compound CNS depression from opioid co-use. Serotonergic opioids can add serotonergic load and respiratory compromise risk when sedation escalates.

Evidence: no direct evidence in controlled trials; interpretation is extrapolated from pharmacology and field guidance.
